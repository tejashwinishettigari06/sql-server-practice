**EXECUTE ORDER**

**-------------**



1. FROM ---

2\. WHERE --- filters data --- removes some rows

3\. Group BY --- combines similar values in one row -- starts aggregating the data based on the aggregate functions

4\. HAVING --- 2nd filtering step --- removes unwanted rows and keeps the rest

5\. SELECT DISTINCT --- selects the columns that we need to see in the result --- once selected, the SQL goes to->

6\. ORDER BY --- data is sorted in ascending or descending order

7\. TOP --- last filtering --- last step in query --- no conditions
